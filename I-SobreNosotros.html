    • Learn More About Us

    Welcome to EcoAdventure Zoo, a haven of biodiversity and environmental education.

    Founded in 1998, EcoAdventure Zoo has established itself as one of the leading wildlife conservation and education centers in the region. Our commitment is to promote love and respect for nature through educational and recreational experiences that inspire all generations.

    Our Mission:

    At EcoAdventure Zoo, our mission is to preserve and protect endangered species through conservation, research, and education programs. We strive to create an environment where visitors can learn about the importance of each species and its role in the ecosystem.

    Our Inhabitants:

    The zoo is home to over 200 species of animals from around the world, including mammals, birds, reptiles, and amphibians. Each of our inhabitants receives the highest quality care from a team of animal welfare experts and veterinarians. Additionally, we participate in international captive breeding programs to help increase the populations of threatened species.

    Environmental Education and Awareness:

    We firmly believe in the power of education to change the world. We offer a wide variety of educational programs, from guided tours and school workshops to specialized talks and summer camps. Our goal is to foster a greater understanding and appreciation of the environment and the measures necessary for its conservation.

    Sustainability and Conservation:

    Sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. We strive to operate ecologically, using sustainable practices in all our operations, from waste management to resource efficiency. Additionally, we collaborate with local and international organizations to carry out conservation projects both within and outside the zoo.

    A Place for the Whole Family:

    EcoAdventure Zoo is a perfect destination for visitors of all ages. Our facilities include play areas for children, picnic zones, restaurants, and souvenir shops. Additionally, we offer interactive experiences such as animal encounters and live demonstrations that make each visit an unforgettable adventure.
